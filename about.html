About Us

Welcome to the MCSM Computer Science Club, a community of students at Manhattan Center for Science and Mathematics who are passionate about exploring the world of computer science.

Founded in the fall semester of 2022, our club provides a supportive environment where students can learn new skills, engage in collaborative projects, and explore the exciting field of computer science.

Our club meets every Monday after school from 3:30 PM to 4:30 PM in room 115 at Manhattan Center for Science and Mathematics. During our meetings, we engage in a variety of activities, including coding challenges, project-based learning, guest speaker sessions, and networking events.

Our members come from a diverse range of backgrounds and skill levels, but all share a common interest in technology and its potential to make a positive impact on the world. We welcome students of all levels of experience, whether you are an experienced programmer or just starting to explore the world of computer science.

Join us to be a part of a dynamic community of students who are passionate about technology and eager to learn and grow together.
